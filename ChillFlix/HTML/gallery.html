ROMANCE | TITANIC

Titanic is a 1997 American epic romance and disaster film. Kate Winslet, who portrayed Rose DeWitt Bukater, and Leonardo DiCaprio, who portrayed Jack Dawson are the two main characters. This is a picture of Titanic sinking scene. It is one of the saddest scenes in the movie and it is one of the most well shot scenes in history. They shooted this scene using a Dutch tilt, a composited waterline that made the ship look more tilted, and various miniatures of the ship.

SCI-FI | ENDGAME

Avengers Endgame is a 2019 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team the Avengers. The final battle of Avengers Endgame was officially called the Battle of Earth in the first episode of the first season of Ms. Marvel. Final fight scene in endgame was in in upstate New York. The Battle of Earth was the ultimate battle for the fate of the planet and the entire universe, fought among the Avengers and their allies united against alternate timeline versions of Thanos, the Black Order, and their full arsenal of Chitauri, Sakaaran and Outrider armies.

FAMILY | THE GOOD DINOSAUR

The Good Dinosaur is a 2015 American computer-animated adventure film produced by Pixar Animation Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. The relationship that slowly developed between Arlo and Spot throughout the journey is the best part of the film. The Good Dinosaur explores similar themes of love, friendship, compassion and courage to overcome life's challenges.
